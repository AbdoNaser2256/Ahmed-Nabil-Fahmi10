Advanced Cosmetic Periodontics

The Art of Radiant Gum Aesthetics.

Gum depigmentation, also known as gum bleaching, uses precision laser technology to gently remove dark spots and restore a healthy, coral-pink appearance to your smile.

CLINICAL PRECISION

State-of-the-art Waterlase™ technology ensures a completely bloodless and painless procedure.

Understanding Pigmentation

info Melanin & Dark Gums

Hyper-pigmentation occurs when an excess of melanin builds up in the gingival tissue. While medically harmless, many patients feel it detracts from the vibrancy of their smile.

Genetic predisposition
Prolonged sun exposure
Smoking-induced melanosis

Our goal is to reveal your natural, healthy coral-pink gums. This contrast enhances the whiteness of your teeth and creates a balanced, harmonious aesthetic that boosts self-confidence.

The Instrument

Elite Laser Vaporization

We utilize specialized soft-tissue lasers that precisely target the melanin layer. Unlike traditional methods, the laser vaporizes the top layer of pigment without affecting the bone or deeper tissues.

spa

Pain-Free Process

No scalpels. No sutures. Just a light topical anesthetic. Most patients report only a mild warming sensation during the 45-minute procedure.

Zero Downtime

Eat and speak normally immediately after your session. The gums heal rapidly, reaching their final radiant shade within 7 days.
