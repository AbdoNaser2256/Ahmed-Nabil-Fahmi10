What is a Root Canal?

It's a specialized procedure aimed at treating the soft core of the toothâ€”the pulp. When this area becomes infected or inflamed due to deep decay or injury, a root canal removes the source of pain while keeping the physical tooth intact.

01

Cleansing

The infected tissue is gently removed using specialized micro-instruments, ensuring total sterilization of the internal canals under local anesthesia.

Sealing

The empty canals are filled with a biocompatible material called gutta-percha, preventing future bacterial re-entry and reinforcing the tooth structure.

Restoration

Finally, the tooth is topped with a custom-crafted porcelain crown. This restores full function and aesthetic beauty, making the tooth indistinguishable from its neighbors.
